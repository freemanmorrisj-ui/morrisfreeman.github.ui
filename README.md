Morris J. Freeman is a Liberian economics professional with academic training in Economics and Banking & Finance, and practical experience in financial operations, administration, organizational management, and customer service. He holds a BSc in Economics from the African Methodist Episcopal University and a Certificate in Banking and Finance from the Banking Institute of Liberia.
Morris has managed financial operations, payroll, procurement, compliance, and customer service as Manager at Bodyzone Fitness and Spa, and previously supported administration and financial tracking at Liberia Finance and Trust Corporation. He also served as Secretary of the Freeport Community Youth of Peace Movement, strengthening organizational records and communication.
Recognized for integrity, resilience, and leadership, Morris aims to advance organizational excellence through financial analysis, strategic planning, and operational management, while contributing to sustainable economic development and community impact.
